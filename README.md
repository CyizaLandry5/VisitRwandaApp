# visitrwanda App

Rwanda, widely celebrated as the "Land of a Thousand Hills," is a premier African destination managed by the official tourism organization Visit Rwanda. Known for its world-renowned primate conservation, dramatic mountain landscapes, and vibrant city culture, the country offers immersive eco-tourism experiences spanning four pristine national parks.
